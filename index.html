ABOUT

The Polytechnique Montréal is an engineering school affiliated with the Université de Montréal in Montréal, Québec, Canada. Polytechnique is a key player in the engineering and innovation sector and a partner of choice for a number of innovative companies, both in Quebec and elsewhere in Canada and around the world. True to its mission since 1873, it has trained more than 50,000 engineers, specialists and researchers.
Hence, it posseses a large and rich dataset of its students, analysing which could be of great use for the ones seeking answers to questions about this university; current and future students, alumni, professors, and bussiness people.
During the last ten years, 195,634 students enrolled

in, and 14,102 graduated from Polytechnique University. Among all of the enrolled students during this time, 26% were women who also formed 24% of the alumni. Also, international students; who have always been welcomed to Polytechnique, comprise 42.5% of the total number of students. Moreover, 71.7%, 17.6% and 10.7% of the students are enrolled in the first, second, and third cycle programs, respectively.
